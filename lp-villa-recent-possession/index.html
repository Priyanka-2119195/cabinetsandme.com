The Process

From Empty Villa to
A Completely Designed Home

Most villa interior projects take 6–12 months because decisions get made late. Our process compresses that without cutting corners.

01
Consultation
At Your Villa
We visit your space, understand your lifestyle, brief and vision. No measurements without understanding. No obligations at this stage.
02
Design
3D Renders of Every Space
Full 3D renders of every kitchen, wardrobe and space. Materials, finishes and hardware presented for your approval before a single piece is made.
03
Manufacture
German Machinery, Bengaluru
Every piece built in our Bengaluru facility. Hettich and Häfele hardware throughout. Quality-checked before it leaves our floor.
04
Installation
Our Own Team
Our team installs — no subcontractors, no open-ended timelines. A precise schedule from day one, with a snag-free walkthrough at the end.
05
Handover
Your Home, Complete
A thorough walkthrough of every piece, every mechanism, every finish. You move in knowing every detail was intended — and built to last.
06
After-Care
Ongoing Relationship
Cabinets & Me maintains a continuing relationship with every client. If anything ever needs attention, our team is a phone call away.
